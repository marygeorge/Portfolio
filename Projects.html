Eat & Burn

'Eat & Burn' is an app formulated to help users stay healthy while they eat their favorite food. It displays the calories in a meal and provide workout options to burn it
Bing web search and nutritionix API play a big part in this app. Front end uses Materialize and Firebase is used at the back end.

Hangman

This is a basic hangman game
Front end have original graphics. Guess the letters of the hidden word to solve the puzzle. Each word can take upto 13 wrong guesses. If you can't solve a word it will be presented to you again at a later stage.
